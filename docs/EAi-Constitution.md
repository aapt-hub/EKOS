# EAi Constitution

EAi Constitution defines the top-level principles of the EAi enterprise engineering platform.

## Principles

- The architecture is the center.
- No module may become the architectural center.
- Everything is generated from the frozen reference architecture.
- Governance is the top-level control surface.
- All work must be capability driven, graph driven, policy driven, contract driven, metadata driven, event driven, governed, observable, and certifiable.
