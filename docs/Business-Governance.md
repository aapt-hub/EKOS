# Business Governance

Business governance ensures EAi can detect and respond to enterprise signals before they become operational loss.

## Coverage

- business capability governance
- business cycle review
- pipeline leakage detection
- retention risk detection
- customer lifecycle tracking
- revenue/process bottleneck detection
- compliance validation
- automation recommendations
- owner notification workflows

## Example

If an accounting company is losing retention, EAi should be able to detect the issue, generate a governed recommendation, and notify the responsible owner through `CONTROLi` and `CONSOLEi`.

## Requirements

- recommendations must be explainable
- recommendations must be auditable
- owner notification must be policy checked
- production changes require human approval
