# Trust Authority

Runtime Trust Authority is implemented in `los/trust/`.

Primary modules:

- `LOS.RuntimeTrustAuthority.psm1`
- `LOS.TrustEvidence.psm1`
- `LOS.TrustDecision.psm1`
- `LOS.TrustLedger.psm1`
- `LOS.TrustReport.psm1`

The authority consumes governance and certification results and produces trust decisions and audit artifacts.
