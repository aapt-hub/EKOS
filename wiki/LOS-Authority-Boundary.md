# LOS Authority Boundary

LOS is the Operating System Truth Kernel and runtime authority boundary for EKOS.

LOS owns:

- Contract truth
- Schema truth
- Attestation truth
- Governance policy
- Certification evidence
- Runtime trust authority
- Runtime trust monitoring
- Runtime trust enforcement

EKOS is the Runtime Execution Engine and must consume LOS authority decisions rather than overriding them.

PETOS is the Application Layer and consumes EKOS/LOS behavior from above the runtime authority boundary.
